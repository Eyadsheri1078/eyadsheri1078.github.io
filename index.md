## ❓ Frequently asked questions

**Does the app charge a fee?**
No. TransCoda4Windows is a free tool.

**Can I stop a conversion in progress?**
Yes. Click the red Cancel button to stop the process immediately. The app will discard any unfinished files.

**Where does the app save my files?**
The app saves files to the folder you select in the output directory box. If you do not choose a folder, it uses your default Videos folder.

**Do I need an internet connection to convert files?**
No. The app performs all conversions locally on your machine. You do not need the internet to process your media.

**What if my video looks bad?**
Check your quality settings in the menu. Increase the bitrate or select a high-quality preset for better results.

**Does this support 4K video?**
Yes. The app handles high-resolution video files provided your hardware meets the requirements.

**Why does the app use my graphics card?**
This process is called hardware acceleration. By offloading video tasks to your graphics card, your computer finishes the work faster and leaves your processor free for other tasks.

**Are my files sent to a server?**
No. Your privacy is protected because all processing remains on your hardware.
